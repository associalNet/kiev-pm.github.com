What

The Perl Ski Hackathon 2013 is an attempt to organize a winter Perl hackathon in Ukraine. Hackathon itself is an event when programmers gather together to hack on something useful (or not), for social purporses and to have a lot of fun.

While the topics may vary usually several groups are formed around the particular interests. The list of proposed topics will be announced later.
